About

Professor of economics at the University of Montpellier (UM), France, since 2014.
Member of the Center for Environmental Economics of Montpellier (CEE-M), since 2019.
Co-responsible for the Master's degree in Economics and Psychology, UM, since 2022.

Research Interests

  • Social choice, social welfare and inequality.
  • Choice under risk, uncertainty and complexity.
  • Experimental and behavioral economics.
  • Education, health and environment.
